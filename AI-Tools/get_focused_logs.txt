Type ESQL

Query

FROM ste-logs-*
| EVAL firstEmbedMessage = CONCAT("*", "", ?message)
| EVAL lastEmbedMessage = CONCAT(firstEmbedMessage, "","*")
| WHERE MATCH_PHRASE(log.message, lastEmbedMessage) AND log.level LIKE ?level
| LIMIT 10

ESQL Parameters
-------------------------------
message

Looks for log message context

text
-------------------------------
level

Get the criticality level of the log, DEBUG, WARNING, WARN, INFO, TRACE, SEVERE, ERROR, CRITICAL,
